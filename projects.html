THE HILLSIDE HOUSE

In Brazil's vibrant city of Santos, this architectural project celebrates the harmony between people and nature, drawing inspiration from the sky, sea, and earth. During this project, I worked alongside an experienced architect and played a crucial role in transforming ideas into reality. From sketch to final delivery, I actively contributed to the modern design, featuring straight lines, large windows, balconies, and captivating stonework. This multi-floor residential home boasts breathtaking views of the sea and city, and its foundation presented a remarkable engineering challenge. A memorable experience early in my career, I worked on the interior design, cabinetries, and landscaping, making this project unique, beautiful, and sophisticated.

Type: Residential Home | Location: Santos, Brazil | Date: 2017

THE CARESS HOUSE

This innovative Urban House project redefines the concept of dwelling by embracing its occupants with a sense of comfort and protection. Inspired by the idea of a hug, the design combines traditional notions of shelter with a new focus on care, especially in light of the COVID-19 pandemic. With meticulous attention to the relationship between the rooms and their occupants, the house caters to all needs, creating a space that fosters safety, well-being, and a strong bond with the surrounding environment. As an architect deeply involved in the creation and development of this project, I am proud to have contributed to this forward-thinking and people-centered design.

Type: Residential Home | Location: College Project | Date: 2021
